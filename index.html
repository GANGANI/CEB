A New Building for Veyangoda Customer Service Center

In order to provide a better and an efficient service to electricity consumers in Veyangoda area, a new building was constructed for the Customer Service Center of Veyangoda. The new building was ceremoniously opened on 30th June 2017 with the presence of Additional General Manager (DD2) Eng. Gratian Muthukudaarachchi, Deputy General Manager (WPN) Eng. Dhammika Thanthirimalage and Chief Electrical Engineer M. D. S. Munasinghe.

Opening of the New Meter Factory at Galigamuwa

The new meter factory for the production of meter enclosures constructed at Galigamuwa factory premises, was declared open by Hon Minster of Power & Renewable Energy Ranjith Siyambalapitiya on 04th September 2017. Hon Deputy Minster of Power & Renewable Energy Ajith P. Perera, Secretary for Ministry of Power & Renewable Energy Dr. Suren Batagoda, Chairman of Ceylon Electricity Board Mr. W. B. Ganegala and many others participated in the event.
